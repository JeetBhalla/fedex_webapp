Key People at FedEx

Frederick W. Smith - Chairman and CEO

As the founder and CEO of FedEx, Frederick W. Smith has revolutionized global logistics, creating one of the world’s most recognized companies. Under his leadership, FedEx has grown into a global powerhouse in transportation and e-commerce.

Raj Subramaniam - President and COO

Raj Subramaniam plays a key role in managing FedEx's operations and driving innovation. As President and COO, he oversees strategic initiatives and ensures the company remains agile in the face of changing market demands.

FedEx Technology and Innovation Team

The FedEx Technology and Innovation team is responsible for developing cutting-edge solutions that improve the customer experience. Their work includes implementing AI, machine learning, IoT, and blockchain for smarter logistics.

Supply Chain and Operations Team

This team is responsible for optimizing FedEx’s global supply chain operations, ensuring timely deliveries, and minimizing costs. They rely heavily on data analytics and advanced algorithms to predict demand and optimize delivery routes.

Cybersecurity and IT Support Team

Dedicated to ensuring the security and integrity of FedEx's systems, this team works tirelessly to protect sensitive customer data and safeguard the company’s IT infrastructure from cyber threats.

Sustainability Leadership Team

The Sustainability Leadership Team at FedEx focuses on environmental responsibility, driving initiatives to reduce the company’s carbon footprint and improve its green logistics operations.
